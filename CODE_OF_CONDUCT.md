# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but address impact directly.
- Focus on technical outcomes and clear communication.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing others' private information.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, commits, code, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
